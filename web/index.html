A LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

卓越性の遺産

Founded in 1989, Kyubi was established by Tsunade Senju, a visionary who embarked on a mission to bring the art of Japanese gastronomy to Granada

Her dedication to authenticity, a commitment to sourcing the finest ingredients, and an unwavering pursuit of culinary excellence set the stage for what would become an iconic dining experience

Over the years, Kyubi has earned a reputation for its unwavering pursuit of perfection.

“Cooking is the art of patience and passion, where every ingredient is a story to be told, and every dish is poetry on the plate”
